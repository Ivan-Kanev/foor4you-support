Recipe photos

Use Camera or Photos to add a main image, then zoom and reposition it before saving.

Favorites

Tap the heart on recipe cards or detail pages to keep your go-to meals close.

Statistics

Track most viewed recipes, meal split, calories, protein, and cooking habits.
